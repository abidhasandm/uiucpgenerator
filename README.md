# UIUCP Generator

Welcome to the UIU Cover Page Generator! This tool helps students of United International University create their lab reports and assignment cover pages quickly and easily. It is completely free and requires no login.

### What the tool offers

✨ Instant Generation
Make your cover pages in seconds instead of formatting Word documents for hours. Enter your details and let the tool do the magic.

💾 Auto Save Feature
The website remembers your information, such as your name and student ID, so you do not have to type it again the next time you need a cover page.

🎨 Beautiful Templates
Choose from six professional designs, including Classic, Modern, Minimal, Professional, Creative, and Elegant. 

👥 Individual or Group Options
Perfect for both your solo assignments and group projects. You can easily add all your group members with a single click.

📥 Multiple Formats
Download your final cover page exactly how you need it in either PDF format or as a Word document.

### How to use it

1. Open the website
2. Choose either Lab Report or Assignment
3. Pick your favorite design template
4. Type in your student information, course details, and teacher information
5. Click Generate Document and download your perfect cover page
